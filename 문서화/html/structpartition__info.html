상세한 설명

The partition information for a single partition.

ASTC has a total of 1024 candidate partitions for each of 2/3/4 partition counts, although this 1024 includes seeds that generate duplicates of other seeds and seeds that generate completely empty partitions. These are both valid encodings, but astcenc will skip both during compression as they are not useful.
